# Activation Energy

## Core idea

Many good ideas disappear because they require too much initial energy to reactivate.

The brain compresses and forgets for efficiency, but that can prune valuable weak signals.

## Related

- [[Meta Neural Network]]
- [[Butterfly Graph]]
- [[Forgotten High Excitement Ideas]]

## Design implication

The system should not only store ideas. It should lower the reactivation threshold.

## Dashboard use

When energy is low, show:

- small dormant ideas
- weekend build candidates
- high-excitement concepts with low next-step cost
