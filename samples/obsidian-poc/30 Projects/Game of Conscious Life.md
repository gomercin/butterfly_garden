# Game of Conscious Life

## One-liner

A simulation where self-preserving patterns emerge and begin to look consciousness-like without requiring magical free will.

## Parent concepts

- [[Harmony Universe]]
- [[Locality of Observation]]

## MVP version

Extend Conway-like rules with:

- energy
- memory traces
- self-preservation pressure
- local perception
- group conflict

## Why it is interesting

It could bridge speculative consciousness ideas with buildable simulation mechanics.
